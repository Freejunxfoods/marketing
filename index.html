About Us

At Freejunx, we believe burgers don’t have to be junk food. Born out of a bold idea — to redefine indulgence without compromise — we serve crave-worthy burgers that are 100% gluten-free, lactose-free, and oil-free. Yes, you read that right. In a world where burgers often mean frozen patties, heavy oils, preservatives, and guilt, we stand apart. Every bite at Freejunx is crafted to satisfy your taste buds while nourishing your body — made fresh, clean, and consciously. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast, a health-conscious foodie, or simply someone who loves a good burger without the bloated aftermath, we’re here for you.
